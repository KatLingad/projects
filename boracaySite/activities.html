Things to do in Boracay

Boracay is well known for its nightlife and the activities during the day.

Nightlife

In station 2, they have nightclubs along the strand connected to the hotels and on the sand, endless sections of entertainment

Daylife

During the day, theres no escapign the affordable activities on the island

Activities range from ATVing, snorkeling, parasailing, island hoping and zip lining.

Just beware of scammers.

Nightlife

There's just so much Boracay has to offer.

If the party scene is not for you, kick back and relax on the beach or by a pool.

Daylife
