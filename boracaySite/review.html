My Experience in Boracay

Boracay has been one of the most beautiful places I have traveled to and the most affordable.

Because of how inexpensive it is to explore and adventure around Boracay, my family and I really got to enjoy ourselves.

Although we only stayed in Boracay for 3 days, we were able to do so many activities.

We were a party of 5 and for about 150 US dollars we were able to paddle board on Day 1 and 2, enjoy a private tour around the island and snorkel in 3 designated spots on Day 2 (snorkel gear included), splurge on full body messages all 3 days, and on the last day went ATVing.

Between all the activities we focused our time on eating and catching up on sleep.
